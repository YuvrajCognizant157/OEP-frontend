FAQ

Quick answers, zero stress

Got questions? We've got simple answers about registering, taking exams, checking results, and more. Let's make your exam journey easy.

How do I sign up for an exam?

Log in, head to Exams, pick your test, and follow the steps to register. It's quick and easy.

How do I begin my exam?

Go to the Exams tab at your scheduled time and hit 'Start Exam'. Make sure your internet is steady.

Can I see my results?

Yes! After you finish, check the Results tab for your scores and any feedback from the team.

How do I share feedback?

After your exam, you'll see a feedback form. Let us know how it went or report any issues there.

Who checks and approves exams?

Admins review and approve all exams and topics before they go live. You'll get a notification when it's done.

How can I add questions?

If you're an examiner, open your exam draft, click 'Add Question', fill it out, and save. Add as many as you need before submitting.
