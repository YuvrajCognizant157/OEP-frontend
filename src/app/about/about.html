Key Features

Exam Builder

Create timed exams with multiple question types, sections, and randomized questions to prevent malpractice.

 Live Proctoring & Security

JWT-based authentication, role guards and configurable proctoring flags keep exams secure and fair.

 Auto & Manual Grading

Automated scoring for objective questions and reviewer workflows for subjective answers with feedback options.

 Detailed Analytics

Item analysis, performance trends, and exportable reports to help improve question banks and student outcomes.

How it Works

1. Create

Examiners build exams with sections, time limits and scoring rules. Save templates for reuse.

 2. Deliver

Students access scheduled exams, complete them on a secure interface, and submit for grading.

 3. Review & Analyze

Automated scores, manual reviews, and analytics dashboards deliver insights and feedback quickly.

Our interface is built to be intuitive and easy to navigate so administrators, examiners, and students can focus on what matters — quality assessments.
